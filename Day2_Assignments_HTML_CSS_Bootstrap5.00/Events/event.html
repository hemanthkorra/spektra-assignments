About the Festival

Celebrate the vibrant festival of colors with us! Holi Festival 2025 brings together people from all walks of life to enjoy a day filled with music, dance, delicious food, and, of course, colors!


Experience live DJ performances, traditional Holi songs, color play zones, food stalls serving festive delicacies, and much more. Join us for an unforgettable celebration of unity, joy, and color!


Don't forget to bring your friends and family for a day packed with fun, laughter, and memories!
